# Requirement Writing Guidelines (Concise)

<!--
source: curated
type: guideline
tags: writing, clarity
version: 2026-03-24
-->

- State the primary outcome and who benefits.
- Specify actors/roles and affected systems.
- Include measurable acceptance criteria and SLAs.
- Note constraints: performance, security, compliance, budgets.
- Identify dependencies and integrations explicitly.
- Clarify environments and rollout plan (staged vs. big bang).
- Define success metrics and observation methods.
- Provide error/edge-case expectations.
- Assign ownership and communication channels.
